# Sample AI Infrastructure Podcast

Host: Today we discuss whether AI inference demand is becoming the main driver of data center buildout.

Guest: Training remains important, but the bigger operational question is inference capacity. Enterprises are moving from demos to production workloads, and that changes the shape of demand. Power availability and interconnect constraints may matter as much as GPU supply.

Host: What would change your view?

Guest: If model efficiency improves faster than application usage, compute demand could disappoint. If usage keeps compounding, power and data center permitting become the binding constraints.
